Rocket League is a vehicular soccer video game developed and published by Psyonix. It was officially released on July 7, 2015.

The event will take place in the SEC Centre - Loch Suite.

The event will be hosted in a bracket style knockout tournament with 10 teams of 3 competing for the grand prize of £5000.

There will be 400 seats that will be availible with the purchase of an event ticket which you can purchase on the Booking tab.

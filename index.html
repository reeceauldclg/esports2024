Event Description

Xtreme Gaming Events © is hosting multiple gaming competitions in the SECC in Glasgow. The event will take place on the 25th and 26th of May 2024 from 10am until 4pm. The competitions are being held in different areas of the area. There will be 500 seats in each area of the arena to watch the competitions. The games played in these competitions will be on Counter-Strike 2, Rocket League and Fortnite.

You can can read more details on each competitions on the Events tab.
You can book tickets on the Booking tab.
You can read find out more about the event on the Information tab.
